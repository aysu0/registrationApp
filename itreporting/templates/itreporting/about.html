Our founding

Lumina University had humble beginnings rooted in a visionary pursuit of academic excellence. Founded in 1985 by a group of forward-thinking educators, industry leaders, and community advocates, the university was born out of a shared commitment to redefine higher education. Inspired by the belief that learning should be a transformative and inclusive experience, the founders embarked on a journey to create an institution that would not only impart knowledge but also nurture innovation, critical thinking, and a sense of global responsibility.

Growth & beyond

Over the years, Lumina University has evolved, adapting to the demands of the modern world while staying true to its founding principles. Today, the institution stands as a testament to the perseverance and foresight of its founders, who laid the groundwork for a university dedicated to the holistic development and lifelong success of its students.
